想法 14

Posted on Jun 27, 2024

To achieve a quality long/semi-year project, there is a methodology that can be followed. The two dimensions that come to mind are planning and tracking. The key is to create a feedback loop and constantly evaluate the progress.

The feedback loop can be structured as follows:

  1. Plan: Develop a detailed plan for the project.
  2. Execution: Implement the plan and work on the project.
  3. Result + Reflection: Assess the outcomes of the project and reflect on the progress made.

The evaluation step can be done on a daily, weekly, and monthly basis to ensure continuous improvement and tracking of the project’s progress.
