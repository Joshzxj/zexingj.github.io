Ask Question Wisely

Posted on Mar 6, 2024

#learning #thinking

What is a Good Question

  • A good question deserves to have a million good answers.
  • A good question cannot be answered right away.
  • A good question will challenge the exist answers.
  • There is no relationship between the question and whether It can be answered correctly.
  • When a good question has been came up with, once you heard, you will have a strong eager to answer it.
  • A good question will expand our mind field.
  • A good question will reconstruct the answer by itself.
  • A good question cannot be predicted.
  • A good question represents a well-educated brain.
  • A good question can derive many other good question.
  • A good question is being the edge of known and unknown, it is neither silly nor trivial.

Step to Propose a Good Question

  • Explain what you are trying to do, not just what you did. This lets your helper know if you are on the wrong track.
  • Specify the point at which the error happens. Does it occur at the very start of the program or only after you do a certain action?
  • Copy and paste the entire error message and your code
  • Explain what you’ve already tried to do to solve your problem. This tells people you’ve already put in some work to fgure things out on your own.
  • If the error came up after you made a change to your code, explain exactly what you changed.
  • Say whether you’re able to reproduce the error every time you run the program or whether it happens only after you perform certain actions. Explain what those actions are, if so.
